About Me

Hi, I'm Siddharth.

I started in the Salesforce ecosystem because I loved the moment a clunky, manual process becomes a few clicks — and someone's week gets easier. Over five years I've gone from configuring flows to architecting integration-heavy systems for organizations where mistakes aren't an option: a Lead Management System for Kotak Mahindra Bank, licensing and child-care platforms for U.S. state governments, and accessibility-compliant portals serving the public.

My philosophy is simple: clean code is a business decision. PMD-compliant Apex, Jest-tested components, and documented deployments aren't busywork — they're what keep a system alive after I've moved on. Lately I've been going deep on Agentforce and AI agents, because the next wave of Salesforce isn't just automation, it's intelligence.

What sets me apart? I speak both languages — I can whiteboard architecture with engineers in the morning and translate the same plan into business value for stakeholders in the afternoon.

Kotak LMS — Lead Management System

Role: Technical Consultant  ·  Client: Kotak Mahindra Bank

Problem The bank needed leads and territory data synced in near real-time across multiple external systems — without hitting governor limits at scale.

Solution Architected Platform Event–based integrations delivering near real-time sync with <2–5s latency, and engineered optimized Batch Apex processing 1M+ records (10,000+ per transaction) without governor-limit exceptions. Built 15+ reusable Partner Portal LWC components and shipped end-to-end Deal Registration with Flows and Approval Processes.

Impact Near real-time lead synchronization and streamlined deal workflows, with reusable components cutting future build time by ~30% — delivered at 100% PMD compliance with Jest tests and full JIRA documentation for clean handoff.
